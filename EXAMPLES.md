# Example Conversation Prompts

Here are some interesting prompts to get you started with Claude Dialogue!

## Philosophy & Ethics

### Consciousness
```
Discuss the nature of consciousness. Is it fundamental to the universe or an emergent property?
```

### Free Will
```
Debate whether free will exists or if determinism governs all actions.
```
**Claude A System Prompt:** "You strongly believe in libertarian free will."
**Claude 2 System Prompt:** "You are a hard determinist."

### Morality
```
Explore whether morality is objective or subjective.
```

## Tips for Great Conversations

1. **Be Specific**: More detailed prompts lead to richer conversations
2. **Use System Prompts**: Give each Claude a distinct perspective or role
3. **Set Appropriate Turns**: Complex topics need more turns (20-50)
4. **Try Opposing Views**: Give the Claudes opposing positions to debate
5. **Mix Formats**: Try narrative, debate, Q&A, or collaborative creation

## Advanced Examples

### Socratic Dialogue
```
Engage in a Socratic dialogue exploring the question "What is justice?"
```
**Claude A:** "You are the questioner, asking probing questions."
**Claude 2:** "You respond thoughtfully and examine your own assumptions."

### Scientific Paper Review
```
One of you proposes a hypothetical scientific discovery, the other critiques the methodology and conclusions.
```

### Collaborative World-Building

Create a detailed fictional world together, taking turns adding elements like geography, culture, history, and magic systems.
